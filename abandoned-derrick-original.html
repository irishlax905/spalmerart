Abandoned Derrick (Original)

Abandoned Derrick is most notable for its thick, impasto technique, contrasted with smoother, blended regions. Some areas of the original piece are tangible, with paint lifted off the canvas. Other areas are smoother, similar to wet-on-wet, blended paintings. The piece depicts an abandoned drilling operation, possibly built in search for oil deposits. The reference photo was taken in the region of Nevada, where many abandoned mines pepper the dry, vast landscapes.

Originals are shipped free anywhere in the USA. Additional shipping fees may apply for international transactions. Your purchase will contain a signed original artwork, sealed in a museum-quality frame (artist's choice) and scratchless, UV-blocking glass, a Certificate of Authenticity and directions on how to hang your new artwork.
