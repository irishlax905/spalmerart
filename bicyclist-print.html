Bicyclist

A signature piece of the artist's Vintage Collection, Bicyclist depicts a Chinese man, cycling home from a long, hard day's work in the rice fields. It was painted from life in photo format in hopes to bring an emotion or a personal memory to surface in association with the bitter sweetness of everyday responsibilities. Japanese produced ink and brush, known as a Sumi-e brush, was used to create the authentic style - emphasized brush strokes, fine details and semi-transparent washes of ink. A Sumi-e brush is extremely versatile, and known to the Japanese to be an all around tool of the art trade.

Framed prints come with glass and are shipped for free anywhere in the US. Poster prints are shipped in mailing tubes. All sales are final.


Authentic Sumi-e Painting
