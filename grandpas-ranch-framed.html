Grandpa's Ranch (Framed)

This is a depiction of Grandpa's Ranch, rich bright green-yellow's along a vignette of cool greens outlined with a weathered wooden fence shouts a quintessential ranch landscape painting. This piece is unique, with a dash of movement and a ton of emotion, it's sure to bring up a conversation or two around the dinner table. You can almost feel the dust that is being thrown from the cruising white pickup truck, come away from the canvas. The subtle but interesting details in the mountain filled background introduces a degree of depth unlike any other piece from the artist. Alongside the wooden fence emcompassing the pasture in the foreground, and the small subject matter in the distance, the harmonous elements deliver a sense of sheer vastness and sense of place.

Framed prints come with glass and are shipped for free anywhere in the US. Poster prints are shipped in mailing tubes. All sales are final.


Where Should I Hang It?

It is proven that Grandpa's Ranch blends phenomenally in a range of arrangements, from rustic sheek settings to mid-century modern/ranch and cabin environments.

Why Should I Purchase?

Grandpa's Ranch is full of emotion and transports viewers into the painting and was one of the first oil paintings the artist completed, a signature piece of the Vintage Collection.
