The Escape from Swiss Cheese Island

The Escape from Swiss Cheese Island is a script written by Stephen Palmer, and intended for animated motion picture. It tells the story of a young mouse, Finch, who is a savvy sailor and learned the trade from his late father, that sacrificed his life protecting his family from pirates when Finch was still an infant. Finch eventually grows up and this results in another run-in with the pirates during a sailing trip, who ends up capturing his girlfriend that he had accompanied on the excursion. Finch frantically chases after the pirates but gets caught in a storm before he could get to them and the storm wrecks his sailboat completely. He wakes up, washed up onto the shores of an island made entirely of Swiss cheese, that he later finds out is a horde of cheese stolen by the pirates, and floating in the ocean like Trash Island. In order to save his girlfriend, he meets an engineering genius, Sal, who helps him carve a new sailboat out of the cheese island. This is the depiction of Finch as he sails away with the boat made of Swiss cheese, with corks in the holes to keep it afloat. Spoiler alert: He saves his girlfriend and defeats the pirates! This illustration was created before the story was written, and was the inspiration for the entire script.
