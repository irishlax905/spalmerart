Stephen Palmer

Variety Oil Painter

Creator of Burt the Worm™, JungleVille™, The Escape from Swiss Cheese Island™, Infinite Travels™ as well as The Carrot Patch™, Stephen Palmer is known as one of the most prolific creatives in the industry. With Stephen's work, you can expect to see a majority of landscapes; especially ones with old weathered wood, followed by still life's and portraits with a touch of illustration and figure study.

Stephen began creating art at a young age by copying doodles of Calvin from Bill Watterson's Calvin and Hobbes, and tracing illustrations out of Shel Silverstein's poem books Light in the Attic and The Giving Tree, after being inspired by a drawing of himself given to him by his father. In his spare time, he enjoys watching cartoons and movies, playing lacrosse, gold panning and walking his dog, Tiki. Stephen aims to capture beauty on a canvas in hopes to bring a memorable emotion to his viewers, young and old.

Art Style and Mission

Stephen has a lot of different art styles but when it comes to oil painting, he prefers to paint with a brush and occasionally, a palette knife. Amongst a wide variety of oil paintings, Stephen also has prints and originals of scratchboard art, watercolor, gouache, acrylic paintings, drawings & illustrations and also pastels.

The Stephen Palmer Gallery aims to bring delightful decor and wonderful additions to each and every setting, humble or commercial, busy or quiet. The wide variety of mediums, techniques and subject matter ensures you will find a piece that really speaks to you. If you don't, reach out to the artist on Instagram and submit a request! On behalf of Stephen Palmer and the Stephen Palmer Gallery, we thank you for your visit.
