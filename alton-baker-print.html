Alton Baker en Plein Air

Painted near the duck and feeding pond at Alton Baker Park on a late October evening, Alton Baker en Plein Air captures quintessential autumn palette in the beginning phases of the Oregon native oak's annual color change. Autumn colors in Western Oregon are most evident in Eugene's University of Oregon campus and Autzen Stadium. The piece focuses on an oak tree overlooking the canal that meanders around Autzen Stadium, and leads to the pond, in the heart of Alton Baker Park. The dynamic reds, oranges and yellows during autumn in wooded regions like the East Coast and Pacific Northwest captivate visitors from all around the world. Now it's your chance to capture the essence of autumn in the comfort of your own home, all you are missing is a pumpkin spice something.

Framed prints come with glass and are shipped for free anywhere in the US. Poster prints are shipped in mailing tubes. All sales are final.
