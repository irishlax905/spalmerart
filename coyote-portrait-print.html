Coyote Portrait

For folks living in Midwest America and surrounding areas, you may have heard the cackling of a coyote from time to time, the wild dogs of America. Although they are fairly hard to spot or otherwise downright reclusive, if you listen closely; especially in the drier regions, you will hear the Yip! Yip! or brief howl of a coyote. To catch a coyote looking at you face to face would be quite the feat to accomplish, so enjoy this Coyote Portrait that captures the essence of these wild dogs of America and all the majesty they stand for, all in one shot of whisky...straight of course, partner.

Framed prints come with glass and are shipped for free anywhere in the US. Poster prints are shipped in mailing tubes. All sales are final.


Where Should I Hang It?

The Coyote Portrait is known to be captivating and is ideal for dormant locations such as hallways or staircases.
