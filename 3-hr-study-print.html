Figure Study: 3 Hrs.

Created in the studios of the academy at the Northwest College of Art and Design where the artist studied Fine Art, the study was a timed drawing; for three hours with a 60 minute intermission. The model depicted has long dreads, tied up with a single dread into a double bun; and held secure with two hands. The model also has distinct tattoos that accentuate the chosen posture. A finishing vignette to the artwork helps to tie it all in, symbolizing a 'window' into the model's world; her emotions, vulnerabilites and strengths - all on the surface, all at once. Celebrate the woman figure with a captivating and authentic live model figure study, created outside of Seattle on a gloomy, autumn's day.

Framed prints come with glass and are shipped for free anywhere in the US. Poster prints are shipped in mailing tubes. All sales are final.


Where Should I Hang It?

Figure Study: 3 Hrs. beautifies any space needing some feminine flare. However, it stands out ideally in commercial settings. Pool halls, night clubs and karaoke bars are all existing venues for the artwork.

Authentic Live Study

All figure studies under the Stephen Palmer Gallery are created with the generous assistance of authentic, experienced live models professionally appointed under the NCAD academy and other resources. Rest assured you will be receiving an authentic live figure study.
