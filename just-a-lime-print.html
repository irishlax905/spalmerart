Just a Lime

Don't let the name fool you - this isn't just any lime, it's a lime with a purpose and a colorful story behind it. This lime once sat on a table in Puerto Vallarta, Mexico, and was utilized not for cooking, but for another reason. In the 1990's, large populated regions of Mexico still suffered from no or little access to clean water and in turn, forced the citizens to result in other ways to hydrate themselves and stay clean. One of the methods to keep their hands clean, for example, was to squeeze some lime juice and coconut water into a small bowl, mix it together and dip their hands in it. The lime juice acts as a natural sanitizer and the coconut water soothes skin from the acidic nature of the lime juice.
